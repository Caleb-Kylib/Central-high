Our Facilities

World-class infrastructure to support holistic development.

Modern Library

Our library is stocked with over 5,000 books, digital resources, and quiet reading corners to foster a love for reading.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology labs allow students to engage in practical experiments and discovery.

ICT Suite

State-of-the-art computer lab with high-speed internet for digital literacy classes.

Adventure Playground

Safe and fun play structures for our younger students to develop motor skills.

Sports Complex

Includes a football pitch, basketball court, and swimming pool for physical education.

Creative Arts Studio

A vibrant space for painting, sculpture, and crafts to unleash creativity.

Music & Performing Arts Hall

Equipped with various instruments and a stage for drama and musical performances.

Central High

Nurturing minds, building futures. Excellence in primary education since 1995.
