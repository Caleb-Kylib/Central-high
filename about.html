About Us

Discover our story, our values, and our commitment to excellence.

Our History

1995 - Foundation

Central High was established with just 20 students and 3 teachers, driven by a vision to provide quality education.

2005 - Expansion

Opened the new wing for Junior Secondary and introduced modern science laboratories.

2015 - Excellence Award

Recognized as the Top Primary School in the region for academic performance and extracurricular activities.

2023 - Digital Transformation

Implemented smart classrooms and a comprehensive digital learning platform for all grades.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence, nurturing global citizens who lead with integrity and compassion.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that empowers every child to reach their full potential through innovative teaching and a supportive community.

Core Values
