Welcome to Central High Primary School

Where every child is valued, challenged, and inspired.

At Central High, we believe in fostering a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

With a rich history of academic excellence and a forward-looking curriculum, we prepare our students to become confident, responsible, and compassionate global citizens.
