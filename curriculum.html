Our Curriculum

A comprehensive learning journey from Play Group to Junior Secondary.

Play Group (2 - 3 Years)

Our Play Group program focuses on social interaction, sensory play, and developing motor skills in a safe and loving environment.

Key Subjects & Activities
  • Sensory Play (Sand, Water, Clay)
  • Music & Movement
  • Storytelling
  • Basic Arts & Crafts
Daily Routine Snapshot
8:00 - 8:30 Arrival & Free Play
8:30 - 9:00 Circle Time
9:00 - 10:00 Guided Activity
10:00 - 10:30 Snack Break
10:30 - 11:30 Outdoor Play
11:30 - 12:00 Story & Departure

Kindergarten (3 - 6 Years)

The Kindergarten curriculum is designed to prepare children for formal schooling by introducing literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills through structured play.

Key Subjects
  • Language & Literacy
  • Mathematical Concepts
  • Environmental Studies
  • Creative Arts
Daily Routine Snapshot
8:00 - 8:30 Assembly
8:30 - 10:00 Learning Centers
10:00 - 10:30 Break
10:30 - 12:00 Numeracy/Literacy
12:00 - 1:00 Lunch & Rest
1:00 - 2:30 Creative Activities

Lower Primary (Grade 1 - 3)

In Lower Primary, we focus on solidifying foundational skills in reading, writing, and arithmetic while encouraging curiosity about the world.

Core Subjects
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • ICT
  • P.E.
Daily Routine Snapshot
7:45 - 8:00 Registration
8:00 - 10:00 Morning Lessons
10:00 - 10:30 Break
10:30 - 12:30 Mid-Morning Lessons
12:30 - 1:30 Lunch
1:30 - 3:30 Afternoon Lessons/Clubs

Junior Secondary (Grade 7 - 9)

Our Junior Secondary program bridges the gap between primary and high school, with subject-specialist teaching and a focus on independent learning.

Curriculum Highlights
  • Advanced Mathematics & Sciences
  • Literature & Creative Writing
  • Foreign Languages (French/Spanish)
  • Coding & Robotics
  • Leadership Development
Daily Routine Snapshot
7:30 - 7:50 Homeroom
7:50 - 10:30 Subject Periods 1-3
10:30 - 11:00 Break
11:00 - 1:00 Subject Periods 4-5
1:00 - 2:00 Lunch
2:00 - 4:00 Sports/Clubs/Study
